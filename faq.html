Frequently Asked Questions

1. What is the return policy for clothing items?

We accept returns within 30 days of purchase, provided the clothing items are in their original condition with tags intact. Simply initiate a return request through our website or contact customer support for assistance. Please see our full Return Policy page for details.

2. Do you offer free shipping?

Yes, we offer free standard shipping on orders above a certain value (e.g., $50) within the country. For orders below this value and for international shipping, charges may apply based on the destination and shipping method selected. Check our shipping information page for current rates and policies.

3. How do I know which size to order?

A detailed size guide with measurements is available on each product page. We recommend measuring yourself and comparing it to the guide for the best fit. If you're between sizes or need further assistance, feel free to contact our customer support team.

4. Are there any ongoing promotions or discounts?

We regularly run promotions and offer discounts! The best way to stay updated is by subscribing to our newsletter at the bottom of the page. You'll receive exclusive offers and be the first to know about sales events.

5. How long does it take to process my order?

Orders are typically processed and shipped within 1-3 business days (Monday-Friday, excluding holidays). You’ll receive a shipping confirmation email with tracking information as soon as your order leaves our warehouse.

6. How can I track my order?

Once your order has shipped, you’ll receive a shipping confirmation email containing a tracking link. You can click this link to monitor your package's journey in real-time. You can also track your order via the 'Order Status' section in your account if you created one.

7. Can I change or cancel my order after placing it?

We process orders quickly, but we'll do our best to accommodate changes or cancellations if the order hasn't been shipped yet. Please contact our customer support as soon as possible with your order number. We cannot guarantee changes once the order is processed.

8. How can I contact customer support?

You can reach our customer support team via email at ahmedbakr@internet.rt, by phone at 1234567, or through the form on our Contact Us page. We aim to respond within 24-48 hours during business days.

9. What payment methods do you accept?

We accept major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express), PayPal, and other secure payment options available at checkout. All transactions are processed securely.
